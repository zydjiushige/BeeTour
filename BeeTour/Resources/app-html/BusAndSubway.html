The price policy of Beijing subway tickets

Beijing subway enters into a new era formally, which means the price of a subway ticket takes 3 yuan at least , since Dec. 28, 2014. According to the adjusted pricing scheme of Beijing rail transit (except airport), the price starts at 3 yuan for the first 6 kilometers. Traveling a distance between 6 and 12 kilometers(including) will cost a passenger 4 yuan, 12 to 22 kilometers(including) cost 5 yuan, 22 to 32(including) kilometers cost 6 yuan. Beyond that, one additional yuan for every 20 kilometers traveled will be charged.

Passengers who spend 100 yuan within a month on public transportation will enjoy a 20 percent discount on exceeded fare prices up to 150 yuan. After reaching 150 yuan, they will see fare prices cut by 50 percent for the exceeded part. the expenditure exceeding 400 yuan can not enjoy any discount.



2.Bus traffic in Beijing

As for buses, the fare for a trip of no more than 10 kilometers is 2 yuan. One more yuan will be charged for every 5 kilometers traveled beyond 10 kilometers.

There are 7 kinds of specific people who can enjoy free rail transit, including People’s Liberation Army soldiers, armed police soldiers, disabled policeman, the blind and the disabled, disabled soldiers, retired cadres and people with courageous act.

There are also 8 kinds of specific groups who take the bus for free, which include volunteers, peole aged 65 years or older, the disabled, People’s Liberation Army soldiers, armed police soldiers, disabled policeman, the blind, disabled soldiers, retired cadres and people with courageous act.

1.Subway Map

The price policy of Shanghai subway tickets

The price starts at 3 yuan for the first 6 kilometers, one additional yuan for every 10 kilometers traveled will be charged.

Discount

Buses and subway Joint ride privilege

Passengers will get a 10% discount for the exceeded fare after spending no less than 70 yuan within a month on public transportation.

Types of the ticket

One-way ticket: One-way ticket is given a certain amount in a specified time and station, which can be used in the rail transit network once.The ticket will be recycled by the ticket machine when the passengers departure the subway station. And it can be resold by the ticket vending machine.

One –day ticket: Shanghai rail transit stations sell one-day tickets at 18 yuan. Once used, you can use it to take all the rail lines infinitely in 24 hours(excluding magnetic levitation railway lines).

There-day ticket: all subway stations in Shanghai sell there-day ticket at 45 yuan. And once used, you can use it to take all the rail lines infinitely in 72hours ( excluding magnetic levitation railway lines).
