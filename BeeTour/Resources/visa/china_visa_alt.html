  • L Visa alters to Residence Permit (for Q Visa Reason)(Special conditions)

    • Basic Documents:

    1. Submit original and copy of the applicant’s valid passport and visa;
    2. Submit the completely filled “ Visa and Residence Permit Application Form” with a recently-taken 2-inch fore headed and full faced photos;
    3. Submit original temporary accommodation registration form (issued by hotels in local city or local police station);
    4. Submit original Physical Examination Record Verification Certificate issued by international travel health care center of Jiangsu province (The applicant who meets 18 years old applies for more than one year valid residence permit for the first time);
    5. Submit kinship explaining letter;
    6. Submit proof material consistent with the applicant’s identity and application reason:
      1. If the applicant visits the local city's residents, he or she must provide original and copy of proof of permanent residence of the visited individual and the resident ‘s identity card;
      2. If the applicant visits residents from other provinces or cities, he or she shall provide original and copy of the visited resident’s Chinese resident id card ,hu kou book, proof of staying in this city more than six months, on-the-job certificate in this city unit or proof of the city house property card;
      3. If the applicant visits overseas Chinese, Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan residents, he or she needs to provide the visited individual’s id card and original and copy of the local Resident Certificate over six months;
      4. If the applicant visits a foreigner resident, you must provide original and copy of the visited foreigner's permanent residence permit.

  • F Visa alters to China Z Visa

    • Basic Documents:

    1. Valid passport and copies;
    2. Provide following papers supporting reasons for alteration application from F visa to Z visa:
      1. Aliens who are chief representatives or representatives of foreign enterprises in China should submit “Registration Certificate of Resident Representative office of Foreign (Overseas) Enterprises” (apply for the first time), “Work Permit of Resident Representative office of Foreign (Overseas) Enterprises”.
      2. Foreign investors in joint-ventures who want to apply for visa alteration should submit enterprise instruments of ratification issued by foreign economy and trade commissions (apply for the first time), duplicates of business licenses and identifications of investments in China.
      3. Foreign corporate representatives, general managers, vice general managers, factory mangers, deputy factory managers or other foreign higher management personnel in joint-ventures who want to apply for visa alteration should submit enterprise instruments of ratification issued by foreign trade commissions (apply for the first time), duplicates of business licenses and “Chinese Work Permit for Aliens” or “Foreign Expert Certificate” or “Chinese Work Permit for Oil Operation on the Sea for Aliens”.
      4. Aliens who are engaged in commercial performances in China should submit instruction from the Ministry of Culture.
      5. Aliens who are accompanying relatives of the above-mentioned persons should submit letters from their enterprises or Chinese competent department stating their relationships.
    3. Visa Alteration Application Form;
    4. One recently-taken two-inch, bareheaded, full-faced photo;
