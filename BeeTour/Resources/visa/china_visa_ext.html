• L Visa(Tourist)Extension

    • Basic Documents for L Visa extension

    Required Documents are as follows:

    1. The passport with the valid China L tourist visa;
    2. Visa Application Form and Residence permit application form（you can get two forms from local Exit and entry administration bureau），with 2-inch passport photo ( white or light blue background) );
    3. The original Nanjing "Registration Form of Temporary Residence"issued by local police station or hotel employer;
    4. Once foreigners have host organization or individual , submit the following documents:
      1. Official letter issued by international travel agency (details including the basic conditions of the applicant and the itinerary schedule, duration of stay, living expenses in China and guarantee to abide by Chinese Law;
      2. the duplicate of the yearly check of business license;
    5. If a foreigner doesn’t have host travel organization or individual, he or she should submit the following documents:
      1. financial certificate of personal living expenses(have certificate to prove personal deposit no less than $3000 in bank )in China;
      2. Hotel reservation, itinerary and airline tickets etc;
    6. Other application materials required by the exit and entry Administration of PSB.

    The procedure for China L visa extension.

    In the Exit and Entry Administration of Public Security Bureau.

    1. Submit passport, visa and credentials;
    2. Fill in Visa Extension Application Form;
    3. Provide related proof materials for extension;
    4. Pay visa and certification fees according to the prescribed rates.

  • F Visa Extension

    • Basic Documents for China F Visa Extension

    Required Documents are as follows:

    1. Valid passport and visa;
    2. One recently-taken two-inch, bareheaded, full-faced photo;
    3. Official letter from the organization;
    4. Visa and Residence Permit Application Form with an official seal;
    5. Foreigner Temporary Residence Form;
    6. Copy of the valid Duplicate of Business License;

    The Procedure for China F Visa Extension

    Go to the Exit and Entry Administration of Local Public Security Bureau

    1. Submit passport, visa and credentials;
    2. Fill in Visa Extension Application Form;
    3. Provide related proof materials for extension;
    4. Pay visa and certification fees according to the prescribed rates.

  • M Visa Extension

    • Basic Documents for China M visa Application

    Required Documents are as follows:

    1. Original valid passport and visa;
    2. The latest original Registration Form Of Temporary Residence (Registration Form Of Temporary Residence);
    3. Two 2- inch photos (4.5 CM x 3.5 CM);
    4. Copy of the company's business license;
    5. Copy of organization code certificate of company;
    6. Original application letter of the company(with official seal);
    7. Company consignment agreement;
    8. Copy of official invitation letter (for entry China)& (The inviting company accordance with the company of visa extension);

    Where can foreigners apply for China M Visa Extension ?

    In the Exit and Entry Administration of Public Security Bureau.

  • Z Visa Extension

    • Basic Documents for China Z Visa Extension Application

    Required Documents are as follows:

    1. Passport with valid visa;
    2. Resume or C.V. (in English or Chinese is ok);
    3. Registration Form Of Temporary Residence (up to date);
    4. Copy of BA degree;
    5. Reference letters from any foreign company to prove you have 2 years full-time working experience (after graduated) in your current field and position;
    6. Employment Contract (oversea assignment contract also works);
    7. Copy of the company Business License;
    8. Copy of the company Enterprise Code License;
    9. Copy of the company Approval Certificate(only for foreign investment company);
    10. Health Certificate;
    11. 5 Passport Photographs (size 4.5cm*3.5cm);

    The procedure for China Z Visa Extension

    1. Step one, go to Human Resources and Social Security Bureau to extend the Employment License;
    2. Step two, go to the local Foreign Experts Affairs Bureau to extend the Foreign Experts Certificate;
    3. Step three, go to the Exit and Entry Administration of Nanjing Public Security Bureau for Work Residence Permit extension.

  • Q Visa Extension

    • Q2 Visa Extension

    Basic Documents for China Q2 Visa Extension

    1. Valid passport or other international travel certificates;
    2. Valid Registration Form of Temporary Residence for visitors issued by police station of them temporary residence or hotels in Nanjing;
    3. Completely filled Visa/ Stay Permit/ Residence Permit Application Form with recent half-length, white background bareheaded, full-faced 2-inch color photo. Please fill in the application form in black ink or sign pen;
    4. A proof of family relationship or kinship, which can be a marriage certificate, a birth certificate or an adoption certificate issued by the relevant national authorities, a kinship certificate issued by the relevant sub-district office, the local police station, the department of human resources of a government unit, or an Embassy in China of their country or the relevant notarization documents etc(Any of above certificates issued by foreign authorities or notary offices needed to be authenticated by the Chinese Embassy or Consulate in the relevant country );
    5. The identification of the person to be visited and a proof of over 6 months’ residence in Nanjing;
    6. The identification document of the visited person and a residence proof of over 6 months’ living residence;
      1. Visiting a Chinese citizen: to submit his /her Chinese Nanjing Household Register or Temporary Residence Permit, and Resident Identification Card:
      2. Visiting a foreigner with a “Permanent Residence Permit for Foreigners”: to submit his/ her “Permanent Residence Permit for Foreigners” and a “Temporary Accommodation Registration Form ” with the validity of over 6 months issued by a local police station or a certificate of over 6 months’ employment issued by his/ her employer in Nanjing;
    7. Other necessary application materials that are required by the Division of Exit – Entry Administration.

    The procedure for Q2 Visa extension
    Go to the Exit and Entry Administration of the Public Security Bureau.

    1. Submit passport, visa and credentials;
    2. Fill in Visa Extension Application Form;
    3. Provide related proof materials for extension;
    4. Pay visa and certification fees according to the prescribed rates.
