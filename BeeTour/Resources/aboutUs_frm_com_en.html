Internship Tour Network Technology Co., Ltd. is a network technology company registered in Shenzhen Qianhai Free Trade Zone. With customized internship practices, experience-type tourism etc. as main products, it provides Internet platform of learning exchanges between Chinese and foreign excellent students and enterprises. It provides enterprises with platform channels and professional solutions for brand image promotion, talent search and reserve and boots promotion and exchanges of Chinese culture in foreign countries.


The services which can be provided by Internship Tour to students：


I. Bilateral matching of students and enterprises：

It selects appropriate internship practice enterprises through matching the demands of enterprises and students, helps students to get to know Chinese enterprises, seeks for employment and entrepreneurship opportunities in China and builds cooperation relationship in the future.


II. Customized internship practice plan：

It designs internship practice plan according to students’ characteristics, preserves, records various data, helps students to improve their own professional skills and realizes the optimal effect of internship practice.


III. Platform interactivity：

It provides global college students with free Internet exchange platform and helps them to have a deeper understanding of China and experience Chinese culture. It also promotes international-talent exchanges and information interaction.


IV. Internship practice records and video records：

It provides excellent talents with whole-process micro-video records, preservation and distribution services and facilitates job selection in the future to be equipped with more detailed and accurate information reference basis.


V. Comprehensive offline service：

It provides students with whole-process offline assistance and services from consultation on coming to China, reception on coming to China, internship practice and tourism to the end of itinerary. It eliminates overseas excellent college students’ extraneous worries for coming to China to take internship practices and a trip.



The services which can be provided by Internship Tour to enterprises:


I. Seeking for elites:

It provides enterprises with channels for international high-end talents search, cultivation and technological introduction. It provides enterprises with talent pool to seek for employers and cooperators in the future. It customizes talent selection, practice and cultivation according to demands, reduces various expenses for enterprises’ targeted personnel recruitment. It brings new thoughts and solutions to enterprises and provides enterprises with more possibilities for upgrading products and technological innovations.


II. Video show:

Internship Tour Platform provides enterprises with whole-process planning and filming services of internship practice, transmits and promotes technologies, products and culture of enterprises to the world through various channels so as to enhance international influence of enterprises and brands.


III. Platform interactivity:

It provides enterprises with free internet exchanges and collaboration platform. It helps enterprise to expand international visions, enhances exchanges and interactions with international excellent talents and boost information exchanges and collaboration among enterprises.

Internship Tour provides convenient online services and thoughtful off-line services. Liaison offices spreading over top-notch universities all over the world are responsible for statistics, screening and recommendation of students’ materials. The domestic professional team provides enterprises with customized solutions of practice cultivation. Professional video cooperation institution provides whole-process video records. Offline service system provides whole-process assistance to overseas excellent college students.

Internship Tour is dedicated to providing students and enterprises with an Internet platform of open exchanges and equal-footed assistance and taking the promotion the international cultural exchanges as its mission. We strive to help global excellent students to experience internship practice in China and assist Chinese enterprise to go global.
